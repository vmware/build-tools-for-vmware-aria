## Overview

The catalog items in {{ products.vra_9_full_name }} consist of different type of content sources - blueprints and workflows.

Blueprints are published to the Catalog automatically every time a new version is created.

Workflows are created in Orchestrator but are not published in Catalog. To publish a specific workflow to the catalog, you need to configure a Workflow content item for it.
